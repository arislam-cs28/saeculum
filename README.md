# Saeculum
## A Fantasy Calendar, Clock, and Timer in one! 

Meet the Saeculum, an idea I got while sitting on the floor and staring at the ceilings, wondering how much time has passed!
Inspired by the Latin word for "era" and typically attributed to a hundred years, the Saeculum is a three-in-one device that does three things: tell you the date; tell time based off an RTC module; and my favorite feature,nuse a potentiometer to set a timer for up to 100 minutes!
